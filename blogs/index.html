Pondicherry Trip!

Pondicherry! First trip after the lockdown!

Summary:

  1. Travel: Bangalore to Pondicherry and back via Bus - 2K
  2. Stay: Ravi Krishna Inn - 1K per night.
  3. Places to visit:
    1. Best South Indian food. Paneer Dosa and Masala tea was the best: Surguru Restaurant
    2. Good 4pm tea: John's Cafe
    3. Bakery: Baker's Street
    4. Beautiful wall for taking photos: Cafe Des Arts
    5. Street shopping: Shopping area
    6. Explore on foot: White town
    7. Golden Temple in Auroville: Matrimandir view point
  4. Beaches to visit[keeping it separate :P]
    1. Good for enjoying sunrise: Rock Beach
    2. A walk along the beach: New sand Beach
    3. Must visit for great photos! Go in the afternoon by 2PM to enjoy the beach. Need to go via a Ferry. Take it from the Chunnambar boat house. Ferry service closes by 4PM on most days! Paradise Beach
